Specialized Neurological Physiotherapy

Dr. Sai Charan is a highly accomplished physiotherapist with specialized expertise in neurological and orthopedic rehabilitation. With a Master's degree in Neurological Physiotherapy and over 4 years of intensive clinical experience, he has dedicated his career to helping patients regain function, mobility, and independence.

His comprehensive approach combines cutting-edge evidence-based treatment methods with personalized care plans meticulously tailored to each patient's unique needs and circumstances. Dr. Charan firmly believes in a holistic philosophy of rehabilitation, addressing not only physical symptoms but also the emotional, psychological, and social aspects of recovery.

We are proud to serve multiple vibrant communities including Suncity, Bandlaguda Jagir, Kalimandir, Kismathpur, Narsingi, and Langerhouse, bringing professional, compassionate physiotherapy services directly to your neighborhood. Convenient home visits are readily available for patients with mobility challenges or transportation difficulties.

Our Treatment Philosophy

🩺 Comprehensive Assessment

Thorough evaluation of your condition using advanced diagnostic techniques to develop a personalized treatment plan tailored specifically to your needs.

🎯 Goal-Oriented Therapy

Setting realistic, achievable milestones with clear timelines to track progress systematically and maintain motivation throughout your recovery journey.

🏠 Home Exercise Programs

Customized exercise routines designed for home practice, ensuring continuous progress and maintaining therapeutic benefits between professional sessions.

📊 Progress Monitoring

Regular comprehensive assessments using standardized outcome measures to quantify improvement and adjust treatment protocols as needed for optimal results.

Our Specialized Services

🧠 Neurological Rehabilitation

Comprehensive treatment for stroke survivors, spinal cord injuries, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and other neurological disorders using advanced neurorehabilitation techniques and cutting-edge therapeutic approaches.

🦴 Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Expert care for fractures, joint replacements, arthritis, chronic back pain, and musculoskeletal injuries with specialized focus on restoring optimal mobility, strength, and reducing pain through targeted therapeutic interventions.

👴 Geriatric Rehabilitation

Specialized programs designed for elderly patients focusing on mobility enhancement, balance improvement, fall prevention strategies, and maintaining independence in activities of daily living for improved quality of life.

👶 Pediatric Physiotherapy

Gentle, effective treatment for children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, and other pediatric conditions using innovative play-based therapy approaches that engage young patients while promoting healing.

⚖️ Balance & Vestibular Therapy

Targeted exercises and specialized therapy techniques to improve stability, prevent dangerous falls, and effectively treat vertigo, dizziness, and other vestibular disorders affecting daily function.

❤️ Cardiac Rehabilitation

Post-cardiac event rehabilitation programs designed to improve cardiovascular health, build endurance safely, and restore functional capacity through carefully supervised, progressive exercise protocols.

⚽ Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Comprehensive sports injury treatment and performance enhancement for athletes of all levels, focusing on safe, efficient return to sport with injury prevention strategies and performance optimization.

💪 Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Structured, evidence-based recovery programs following surgical procedures to optimize healing processes, minimize complications, and restore function efficiently with personalized care protocols.
